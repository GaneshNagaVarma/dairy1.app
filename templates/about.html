About Fresh Valley Dairy Farm

Our Story

Fresh Valley Dairy Farm has been serving the community with premium quality dairy and meat products for over 50 years. Founded in 1970 by the Johnson family, we've maintained our commitment to sustainable farming practices and animal welfare.

Our farm spans over 500 acres of lush green pastures where our happy cows graze freely. We believe that happy animals produce the best quality products, which is why we prioritize their comfort and well-being.
